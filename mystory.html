Here is A little thing to know about me.

My name is Lamin M.K Suwareh. I was born on 3rd May in Brikama Nyambai, West Coast Region, The Gambia. I grow up in Jamisa and currently living in Jalanbang. I did my Primary school at Presentation of Mary Basic School and upon completing grade 6, I encountered an illness which resulted giving me hearing loss. After encountering the illness, I skipped school for a year seeking medication from both traditionally and modernly and fortunately before resuming school the following academy year, I was able to hear sound in my right ear.

I was then taken to Saint John’s school for the deaf and hard of hearing to proceed my education there but after conducting test on me, they decided that I wasn’t qualified to learn with them because I was hearing some sounds in my right ear and also speaking fluently. Thus, I was given a hearing-aid to use in school. I went back to my former school and continue my education with abled people. But unfortunately before I complete grade 7, I lost hearing in my right ear too, thus I became completely deaf. I stopped using the hearing-aid and continued my education through my talent of lip reading and ability to read, write and understand English.

I did the rest of my junior school in that situation and with the determination, I sailed through the obstacles. I sat my grade 9 examination and passed it in flying colors. I moved to Gambia senior secondary school where I studied pure science and sat the school leaving certificate examination but missed oral English exam due to my condition.

I later move to Gambia Technical Training Institute (GTTI) where I studied computer science information technology. I learned with the abled people there too since I didn’t learn sign language. I believe I can do whatever the abled people can do because Disability does not tantamount to Inability and as long as there is will, there is always a way. I am Currently a Frontend Developer @assutech.

I want to do my fight not by telling people but by showing them that I can do it. I have 1 disability, but 99 abilities.

By Lamin M.K Suwareh.
8th December, 2022.
